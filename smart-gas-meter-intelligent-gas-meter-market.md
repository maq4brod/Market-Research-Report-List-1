<p><p>Smart gas meters, or intelligent gas meters, enhance gas consumption monitoring through advanced technology. The market comprises two main types: Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems allow for remote data collection at scheduled intervals, improving efficiency and reducing manual readings. In contrast, AMI encompasses a broader range of functionalities, enabling real-time data transmission, two-way communication, and integrated smart grid capabilities, facilitating better energy management and customer engagement in the gas distribution sector.</p></p>
