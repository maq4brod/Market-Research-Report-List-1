<p><p>The liquid butter alternatives market is primarily categorized into two types: palm oil and lauric oil. The palm oil type is derived from palm fruit and is valued for its stability at high temperatures, making it suitable for cooking and baking. Lauric oil, on the other hand, is extracted from coconuts and palm kernels, known for its health benefits and creamy texture. Both types cater to the growing demand for healthier, plant-based alternatives to traditional butter, appealing to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
