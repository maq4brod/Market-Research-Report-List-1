<p><strong>The X Ray Flat Panel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray flat panel sensor market comprises two main types: indirect and direct. Indirect type sensors utilize a scintillator to convert X-ray photons into visible light, which is then transformed into an electronic signal through a photodetector. This approach tends to offer higher sensitivity and image quality. Direct type sensors, on the other hand, directly convert X-ray photons into electric charges using a photoconductive material, enabling faster image acquisition and reduced noise, catering to various clinical applications.</p></p>
