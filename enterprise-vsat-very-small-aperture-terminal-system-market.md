<p><p>The Enterprise VSAT market consists of three primary types based on network architecture: Star Structure, Mesh Structure, and a combination of both. </p><p>Star Structure features a central hub that connects multiple VSAT terminals, providing efficient communication and management. </p><p>Mesh Structure allows direct peer-to-peer links between terminals, enhancing redundancy and reducing latency. </p><p>The Star and Mesh Structure combines both approaches, leveraging the benefits of centralized management and decentralized communication, making it suitable for diverse operational needs and improving overall network resilience.</p></p>
