<p><strong>The USB Power Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Average Power / CW</li><li>Peak Power</li><li>Pulse Power</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB power sensor market encompasses various types based on power measurement capabilities. Average power sensors measure continuous power over time, often used in steady-state applications. Continuous Wave (CW) sensors track power in signals that do not fluctuate significantly. Peak power sensors capture the highest power level in short bursts, ideal for pulsed signals. Pulse power sensors analyze power over time for short-duration signals, focusing on energy in each pulse. Other types may include specialized sensors for unique applications.</p></p>
