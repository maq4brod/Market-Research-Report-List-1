<p><p>The silicone thoracic catheter market is categorized into two main types: straight silicone thoracic catheters and right-angled silicone thoracic catheters. Straight silicone thoracic catheters are designed for direct access to the pleural space, providing a straightforward approach for fluid drainage. In contrast, right-angled silicone thoracic catheters feature a bend in their design, facilitating easier insertion in challenging anatomical situations or for specific drainage angles. Both types are essential for effective thoracic interventions, offering varying benefits based on clinical needs.</p></p>
